Angkor Wat

Cambodia

About Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is the largest religious monument in the world, located in Siem Reap, Cambodia. It was originally constructed as a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu, but gradually transformed into a Buddhist temple. Visitors can explore its intricate carvings, towering spires, and the surrounding historical complex.

Top Activities

  • Explore the temple complex at sunrise
  • Visit the Angkor National Museum
  • Take a guided tour to learn the history and architecture
  • Enjoy local Cambodian cuisine nearby

Best Time to Visit

November to March (cool and dry season)

Nearby Attractions

  • Ta Prohm Temple
  • Bayon Temple
  • Siem Reap Night Market
  • Floating Villages of Tonle Sap Lake
