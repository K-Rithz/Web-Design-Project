About Tokyo

Tokyo is Japan's bustling capital, blending ultramodern skyscrapers with traditional temples and historic neighborhoods. From neon-lit streets to peaceful gardens, Tokyo offers a unique travel experience.

Top Things To Do

  • Visit Shibuya Crossing
  • Explore Asakusa and Senso-ji Temple
  • Enjoy city views from Tokyo Tower
  • Experience Japanese street food

Best Time to Visit

March-May (Cherry Blossom season) and October-November (Autumn)

Nearby Attractions

  • Mount Fuji
  • Hakone
  • Nikko
